About Me

Jacob is a designer and developer based in Long Beach, California. He's interested in connected devices and user experiences that are useful, usable, and fun.

He has a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Southern California, a Nanodegree in Virtual Reality from Udacity, and a Specialization in Web and Mobile Develpment from Coursera. He's currently working towards certificates in Web Development from the University of California Irvine and User Experience from Springboard. He's addicted to learning and hopes to teach someday.
